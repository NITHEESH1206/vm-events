Who We Are

Where Every Celebration
Becomes a Story

We began with a simple belief — that no two celebrations should ever feel the same.

VM Events & Decorations was founded in 2017 in the heart of Ooty by a small team of decorators who fell in love with the way the Nilgiri mist softens every memory. What started as floral styling for hometown weddings has grown into a full-service celebration studio crafting hundreds of events a year.

Today our team of fifteen designers, chefs, photographers and coordinators carries one promise: to treat your event like the only one on our calendar.
